# Botium Toys — Internal Security Audit

**Module:** Foundations of Cybersecurity (Google Cybersecurity Certificate — Course 2)
**Type:** Security Audit & Compliance Assessment
**Status:** Completed

---

## Overview

This project documents an internal security audit conducted for **Botium Toys**, a fictional small U.S.-based toy company with a growing online presence. As the company expanded its operations internationally, the IT department recognized the need to assess and improve its security posture to meet compliance requirements and reduce risk.

The audit was performed using industry-standard frameworks including the **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)** and follows the structure of a professional security audit engagement.

---

## Business Context

Botium Toys processes online payments and stores customer data, including information belonging to customers in the European Union (EU). The company lacks a formal security plan, and the IT manager requested a scope review and risk assessment to identify gaps before regulators or bad actors do.

---

## Audit Scope & Goals

- **Scope:** Entire security program at Botium Toys — all assets, internal processes, and procedures related to the implementation of controls and compliance best practices
- **Goals:**
  - Assess existing assets and identify controls that need to be implemented
  - Determine which compliance standards apply and identify gaps
  - Provide actionable recommendations to improve the security posture
