## My Professional Statement

I am an aspiring cybersecurity analyst with a strong commitment to protecting organizations and the people they serve. My interest in cybersecurity stems from a desire to understand how systems are attacked and how defenders can stay one step ahead.

Through the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate, I am building hands-on skills in security auditing, network analysis, incident response, and compliance frameworks. I bring analytical thinking, attention to detail, and a methodical approach to problem-solving — qualities that are essential for identifying and responding to security threats.

My values of integrity, continuous learning, and service align with the mission of any security team: to protect sensitive data, maintain trust, and ensure systems remain available and reliable. I am committed to growing into a Tier 1 SOC Analyst role where I can contribute to an organization's security operations while continuing to develop my technical expertise.
